Hello everyone!
Welcome to my choose your own adventure story, in this story you will play as a person called Placeholder as they go through a day in their life from start to end.
How this works is there will be some text describing what is currently going on, then you will be presented with 2 or 3 options, select one of these options to continue the story.
There are 9 endings, can you get them all?!
